The Ford Mustang is an iconic American muscle car known for its sleek design, powerful performance, and rich heritage. First introduced in 1964, the Mustang quickly became a symbol of freedom and speed, capturing the hearts of car enthusiasts worldwide. With its aggressive front grille, aerodynamic curves, and roaring engines, the Mustang blends classic styling with modern engineering.
